THIS IS NUMBERCRUNCH

From the co-creators of the hit sensation Bloodlines, NumberCrunch is the newest fast-paced, block-stacking, brick-breaking game that is taking the world by storm. Your objective is to score as many points as you can by stacking blocks on top of each other. But be careful not to place a block in the wrong spot!

RULES

NumberCrunch is easy to learn, but takes a lifetime to master. The aim of the game is simple. Blocks will fall randomly from the top of the screen, and you must drag them onto 1 of 6 stacks. The thing is, only blocks that are of the same number or smaller can be placed on the top of a pile. For example, a 4 can go on a 4 or a 5, but not on a 3.

SCORING

To score points, finish a stack by placing a 1 on top. Every block that the 1 hits will be destroyed and its value will be added to your score. However, any block that is placed illegally will be subtracted from your score. So be careful where you place your blocks!
